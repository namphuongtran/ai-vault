**Stage 0** is the foundation layer. The goal here is breadth, not depth: understand what
each concept *is* and *when* to use it. We go from foundation to advanced, and deeper
explanations come in later stages.

## In this section

1. **Foundation models** — large pre-trained, general-purpose models.
2. **Generative AI** — models that create new content.
3. **Prompt engineering** — how to control a model's output.
4. **RAG** — grounding answers in external data (embeddings + vector stores).
5. **Agents** — models that plan and take actions with tools.
6. **Guardrails** — safety and control around inputs and outputs.
7. **Model evaluation** — how to measure quality for a given task.
8. **Responsible AI** — fairness, transparency, and human oversight.
